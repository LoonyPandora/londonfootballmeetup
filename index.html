Where We Play

Regent's Park

Sundays and Saturdays, rain or shine, we'll be in Regent's Park playing football.

We meet up by the house icon (Regent's Park Tube Stop), then move to the player icon to get our game on.

After the game we got to the pub for a well earned refreshing beverage.

Stratford

Sometime midweek we play indoor 6-a-side at Stratford.

It's different to the weekend sessions as space is limited and it costs £5 to book the hall. you must reserve your place in the signup thread

Keep an eye out for the signup thread, and be quick if you want a place!
